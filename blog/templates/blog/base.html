About

This is a blog made by Ayman for the students of the class of 2024 of Harare International School for the purpose of using this blog as a way to share thoughts on different topics among the students of the class

Getting started

To get started you can do the following things

  • First you can go to the register page from the navbar and then create an account
  • Then you can work on your profile and maybe add a profile picture
  • Next you can check out other posts in the blog
  • You can then like posts and comment on them
  • If you need you can update or delete any comments you made
  • You can also create your own posts and update or even delete them
  • If you ever forget your password you can go to the forgot password link
  • If you ever need any help, have any queries or suggestions, you have a contact page where you can contact support
